# Legends Tier — Combo Expansion
## For Claude Code — add all 20 to the LEGENDS tier in the DRILL tab

Each combo needs: name, combination notation, coaching cue.
Body shots use B prefix (B1 = body jab, B2 = body cross etc.)
Defensive moves: SLIP, ROLL-L, ROLL-R, OUT

| Name | Combination | Coaching Cue |
|---|---|---|
| The Ali Shuffle Entry | 1-1-2-3-2 | Double jab to create confusion, cross-hook-cross to finish. Stay light on your feet. |
| The Tyson Peek-a-Boo | 1-SLIP-2-3 | Jab to draw the counter, slip outside, cross and hook together. Get in close. |
| The Sugar Ray | 1-2-ROLL-L-3-2 | Jab-cross, roll under the hook, come back with hook-cross. Rhythm is everything. |
| The Frazier Pressure | 1-2-B2-3 | Standard opener, body cross to drop the guard, lead hook upstairs. Keep moving forward. |
| The Pacquiao Blitz | 1-2-3-2-3-2 | Six punch combination at full pace. Every punch sets up the next. Don't stop. |
| The Mayweather Counter | SLIP-2-3-2 | Slip the jab, shoulder roll into cross-hook-cross. Defence into offence. |
| The Foreman Closer | 1-2-B3-2 | Establish range with jab-cross, body hook to bend them, finishing cross. Power through. |
| The Leonard Feint | 1-1-SLIP-2-5-3 | Double jab feint, slip your own line, cross-lead uppercut-hook. Catch them guessing. |
| The Hagler March | 1-B2-3-B4-2 | Alternate head and body throughout. Never stay on one level. Keep walking them down. |
| The Hopkins Smother | 1-2-ROLL-R-B2-3 | Jab-cross, roll away from the counter, body cross and hook. Control the distance. |
| The De La Hoya | 1-2-3-SLIP-2-3 | Classic combination, slip outside, double up on the finish. Stay balanced throughout. |
| The Duran Brawl | B1-B2-3-2 | Come in low with body jabs and crosses, hook upstairs to straighten them up, finishing cross. |
| The Calzaghe Volume | 1-2-3-4-1-2 | High output combination. Speed over power. Keep the pressure constant. |
| The Khan Entry | 1-1-2-ROLL-L-3-2-3 | Double jab to get in range, cross, roll under, three punch finish. Explosive start. |
| The Liston Stalk | 1-2-B3-B4-2-3 | Systematic body work to break down the guard, finish upstairs. Stay patient. |
| The Hearns | 1-2-3-2 | Simple but thrown at maximum power. Every punch a threat. The right hand ends it. |
| The Ward Control | 1-SLIP-B2-3-2 | Jab to establish, slip, go to the body, come back upstairs with hook-cross. Surgical. |
| The Chavez Cut Off | 1-2-B2-B3-2 | Use the jab to cut off the ring, attack the body to slow them down, finish with the cross. |
| The Canelo Switch | 1-2-ROLL-L-B3-2-3 | Jab-cross, roll under the return, body hook, cross, lead hook. Read their reaction. |
| The Championship Round | 1-1-2-3-2-5-3-2 | Eight punch combination. Save this for when you're fit and fast enough to throw it clean. |
