PROGRESSION MODEL
Weeks 1–2
Learn the sessions. Focus on form and rep quality. Don't chase weight yet.
Weeks 3–4
Add 2.5–5kg to main lifts where you completed all reps cleanly.
Week 5
Deload. Drop to 60% weights, cut sets by one. The app will flag this automatically.
Weeks 6+
Repeat and reload. Rotate sessions for variety and fresh stimulus.
COACH'S NOTES
—Your boxing classes ARE your cardio base. Don't add more unless under-trained.
—Eat enough protein. 1.6–2g per kg of bodyweight daily. Non-negotiable for the aesthetic goal.
—Sleep is your third training session. Aim for 7–8 hours.
—The trap bar replaces conventional deadlift on Top Down when it arrives — more knee-friendly, loads heavier safely.
